The Problem

Students struggle with maintaining healthy lifestyles due to time management challenges and academic pressure. Many students experience poor sleep, inconsistent diets, and increased stress levels.

Current tools such as calendars and reminder apps are disconnected, making it difficult to understand how lifestyle factors influence academic performance.

Our Goal

The Student Wellness Tracker aims to provide a centralized platform where students can monitor sleep, nutrition, assignments, and stress levels to build healthier habits.
